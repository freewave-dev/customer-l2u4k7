About the Firm

Island counsel you can reach

Orcas Law serves Eastsound and the wider Orcas Island community with a focused practice: real property, probate, and estate planning. Founded by attorney Derek Mann, the firm was built on a simple idea: islanders deserve capable legal help close to home, from someone who knows the place they live.

Every matter is handled with care and plain language — no phone trees, no shuffling between associates. When you work with Orcas Law, you work directly with your attorney.

Derek Mann, JD

Founding Attorney, Orcas Law

  • Juris Doctor · Member, Washington State Bar
  • Real property, probate & estate planning for Orcas Island & San Juan County
  • Direct, one-to-one client relationships

Our approach

We favor negotiation and mediation over drawn-out conflict wherever we can — protecting relationships and keeping costs down. We translate the legal fine print into clear choices, so you always understand where you stand and what comes next.
