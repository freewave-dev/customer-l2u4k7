Civil Litigation

Most disputes on a small island are with people you’ll see again — at the market, in the ferry line, at school pickup. We resolve conflicts with that reality in mind: firmly, but with an eye toward what comes after.

When agreement isn’t possible, we represent clients in mediation, arbitration, and in San Juan County Superior Court.

How we can help

  • Property, boundary & easement disputes
  • Contract disputes
  • Construction disputes
  • Landlord–tenant matters
  • Mediation and arbitration
  • Judgments and enforcement

What to expect

  1. Assessment — the merits, the cost, and the realistic outcomes — stated plainly before you spend a dollar.
  2. Resolution first — demand, negotiation, and mediation where they serve you; most matters end here.
  3. Litigation — prepared thoroughly and tried properly when nothing else protects your position.
