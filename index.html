About the Firm

Island counsel you can reach

Orcas Law is a general-practice firm serving Eastsound and the wider Orcas Island community. Founded by attorney Derek Mann, the firm was built on a simple idea: islanders deserve capable legal help close to home, from someone who knows the place they live.

Every matter is handled with care and plain language — no phone trees, no shuffling between associates. When you work with Orcas Law, you work directly with your attorney.
